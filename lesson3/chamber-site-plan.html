Site Name: Snowflake AZ Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Welcome to the official Snowflake Chamber of Commerce website. Our purpose is to connect, inform, and empower the residents and businesses of Snowflake. As you explore our website, you'll find attention-grabbing information, history, demographics, and events on the Discover page, we are your one-stop source for all things Snowflake. Our Directory section lists local businesses and organizations proudly belonging to our Chamber, while our Join page presents the benefits of membership, whether you're a non-profit, silver, or gold-level supporter. Explore, connect, and thrive with us here at Snowflake's Chamber of Commerce website.

Senarios

Scenario 1 - Event Information

Visitor Persona: Local Resident Interested in Chamber Events

Question: "I'm a local resident and heard about upcoming events organized by the Snowflake Chamber of Commerce. Where can I find information about these events, including dates, venues, and registration details?"

Scenario 2 - Contacting the Board of Directors

Visitor Persona: Community Member Interested in Chamber Leadership

Question: "Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?" In this case, the website should have a clear and accessible directory of the board of directors, including names, positions, email addresses, and perhaps short bios. This information should be available on the Directory page or in an About Us section.

Scenario 3 - Area Population Growth

Visitor Persona: Prospective Resident or Business Investor

Question: "What has been the population growth in the Snowflake area?" To address this inquiry, the Discover page should contain information about the town's demographics, historical population data, and trends. Visual aids like charts or graphs could help illustrate this data effectively.
