White Space and Clean Design:

Organization/Company: Apple

Website: Apple

apple.com

Description: Apple's website is a prime example of clean and minimalistic design with ample white space. The homepage features a simple layout with high-quality product images and concise text. The use of white space allows the products to stand out, creating a visually pleasing and clutter-free browsing experience.

Visual Hierarchy:

Organization/Company: Google

Website: Google

google.com

Description: Google's homepage exemplifies visual hierarchy through its minimalist and focused design. The most prominent element on the page is the search bar, which is large and centered. This immediately draws the user's attention and signifies that the primary action on the page is to perform a search. The Google logo, although colorful, is visually subordinate to the search bar, reinforcing the importance of search.

Hick's Law:

Organization/Company: Amazon

Website: Amazon

amazon.com

Description: Amazon's website embraces Hick's Law by offering a wide range of products and options while keeping the user experience relatively simple and intuitive. The homepage presents users with clear navigation menus, prominent search functionality, and personalized recommendations, reducing decision-making time for users looking for products to purchase.
